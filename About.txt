This file contains information about The Students Catalog.
Here you can find what you are interested in. :)

Here you can add new people by entering the "add people" command, followed by the full name and the age of the person you want to add.
Example: "add person Alexia Hincu 18"
If the person you want to add already exists, you will be informed.

Use the "find person" command, followed by the name of the person you want to find in the catalog.
Example:
For person Alexia Hincu, 18, you can type:
"find person Alexia"
"find person Hincu"
"find person Alexia Hincu"

If you want to see all the people from the catalog, you can simply enter the command "list persons".
